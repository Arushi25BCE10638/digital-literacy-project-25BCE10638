 TASK-1 REPORT

 

TOOL USED:

•	The infographic titled "Digital Literacy Guide" was designed using Canva, a versatile tool that allows creators to blend professional aesthetics with structured information through a drag-and-drop interface.



WHAT THE DESIGN SHOWS:

•	The design utilizes a road map layout, featuring a winding path that guides the viewer through five critical steps of digital fluency. 

•	Set against a high-contrast dark blue and white colour scheme, the visual hierarchy is clear; large numerical markers and clean icons (like the pencil for tools and the shield for safety) make the complex topic feel accessible and sequential.

•	The guide effectively breaks down the digital journey, starting from a foundational definition of digital literacy and moving into practical applications like student tools, internet safety, professional branding, and email etiquette.



INTERESTING AND DIFFICULT ASPECT:

•	One particularly interesting aspect of this design is the use of the winding path as a metaphor for a learning journey. 

•	While visually engaging, it also presents the most difficult design challenge: maintaining alignment. 

•	Ensuring that the text blocks and circular icons remained perfectly centered and proportional along the curves of the road requires meticulous attention to spacing. 
•	Balancing the dense information in sections like Safe Internet Practices without making the path feel cluttered is a subtle but impressive feat of layout management.
